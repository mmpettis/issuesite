Mental Health in High School Students

Why We Care

Mental health effects people at all stages of life. However, in adolescence there are extra stressers that contribute to a decline in student mental health.

These may include:

  • Cyberbullying
  • Pressure to succeed
  • Anxiety

One in six U.S. students ages 6-17 experience a mental health disorder each year. Undiagnosed and untreated, this can effect students' ability to learn, develop, and grow. As individuals who interract with students, we must be charged with protecting and providing positive environments.
